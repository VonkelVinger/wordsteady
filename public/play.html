Today’s word

Steadfast

Meaning: firm and reliable in purpose, loyalty, or belief

Example:
“She remained steadfast during the crisis.”

Step 1: Notice the word

Say the word out loud once or twice. Pay attention to how it looks and sounds. Do not rush.

Step 2: Write it correctly

Write the word steadfast five times. Focus on spelling it correctly each time.

Step 3: Use it

Write one sentence using steadfast that you could realistically say or write yourself.

That’s it. Short, focused practice done consistently is what builds vocabulary that actually sticks.
